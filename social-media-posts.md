**Newsletter #1 — Launch Edition**

Subject: Introducing WhatsUp SanLee — Your Community's Digital Home

---
Dear Sanford & Lee County Community,

For too long, finding out what's happening locally has meant scrolling through scattered Facebook groups, driving past hand-written signs, and relying on word of mouth. We knew there had to be a better way.

Today, we're excited to introduce **WhatsUp SanLee** — a free, community-first platform built exclusively for the people of Sanford and Lee County, North Carolina.

**What is WhatsUp SanLee?**

Think of it as your digital town square. A single place where you can:

**Discover Local Businesses**
From food trucks to artists, photographers to farmers — find and support the people who make Lee County special. Every listing includes contact info, photos, schedules and even online booking.

**Stay Connected to Your Community**
Never miss a community event, yard sale, or farmers market again. Our events calendar and community feed keep you in the loop on everything happening in your neighborhood.

**Family Activities Hub**
We know how hard it is to find things to do with kids in Lee County. That's why we built a dedicated Family Activities Hub featuring parks, playgrounds, hiking trails, and family-friendly events — all in one place.

**For Local Businesses — It's Free**
If you run a food truck, farm, photography business, or art studio in Lee County — your listing is completely free. No monthly fees. No commissions. We believe local businesses deserve a platform that puts them first.

**We're Just Getting Started**

WhatsUp SanLee is currently in public beta, which means we're actively building and improving based on YOUR feedback. Coming soon: a Local Digital Creators Directory, push notifications, and a mobile app.

Visit us at **whatsupsanlee.com** and create your free account today. And if you have ideas, feedback, or just want to say hi — reply to this email or join our Discord community.

Together, let's build something great for Lee County.

With community love,
The WhatsUp SanLee Team

# 💡 POSTING TIPS BY PLATFORM

**Facebook:** Post 4-5x per week. Use longer captions. Tag local businesses when possible. Join Lee County community groups and share there too.

**Instagram:** Post 5x per week. Use all 30 hashtags. Stories daily — behind the scenes, polls, countdowns. Reels get 3x more reach than static posts.

**TikTok:** Post 3-4x per week. Hook in first 3 seconds is everything. Show the actual website — screen recordings work great. Trending sounds boost reach.

**Nextdoor:** Post 1-2x per week max (don't spam). Hyper-local tone — mention specific neighborhoods. Great for yard sales and family events.

**X/Twitter:** Post 2-3x per day. Short and punchy. Retweet local accounts. Engage with replies.

**Substack:** Weekly newsletter. Highlight 1 local business or community member each issue. Build the email list — it's your most owned audience.

**Bluesky:** Growing platform. Same strategy as Twitter. Good for reaching tech-forward community members.
